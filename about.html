About

Surakshya's Design is an Architecture firm founded in 2014 by a Group of Architects, Engineers and Urban Planners Graduated From Pulchowk Engineering College, Nepal. Our Team Comprise of Experts in the Field of Architecture, Engineering, Interior Design, and Urban Planning. We Provide Architectural and Engineering Consultation, Interior Design, and Turnkey Project Development all over Nepal. We are Registered Architects, Registered in Nepal Engineering Council, Society of Nepalese Architects and Regional Urban Planners Society of Nepal. Our office is located at Jhamsikhel, (Near Arun Thapa Statue, 3 min walk from British School, 5 min Walk from Chaudhary Group office), Lalitpur. We are in the field of design and Construction of Luxury Residential Bungalow with Swimming Pools, Restaurant Interior Design, and Complete Architectural/ Engineering design of Hospitality Facilities such as Resorts and Landscape Design as well as Design and Construction of Commerical office complexes and Institutions.

PRE-DESIGN This is the FIRST PHASE.

Voxel’s Architect Team will meet with Owner to discuss Project Requirements. Voxel will Sign Agreement with the Owner for Design Services. Voxel’s Architect Team will Analyze & Research project requirements to establish the framework of the project. Voxel’s Architect Team will present a Detailed Program, Conceptual Notes and Schematic Design.

DESIGN This is the SECOND PHASE

Voxel’s Architect Team will improve upon schematic design incorporating Owners Feedback Voxel’s Architect Team will present Design Development (Illustrations, Videos, Photos, Notes). Voxel’s Architect Team will sign agreement with Structural Consultants, MEP Consultants & HVAC Consultants Voxel’s Architect Team will further improve on Design Development after input from Consultants. Voxel’s Architect Team will present A final Design Development after Reviewing to the Owner. Final Municpal Drawings, Working Drawings, Construction Documents, Material Specification, Cost Estimates is Presented.

CONSTRUCTThis is the THIRD (FINAL) PHASE.

After the DESIGN Phase is completed, Construction Process Begins. Voxel Can be involved in Total Construction Managment or Supervision Roles only as per owner’s needs.
