PRE-DESIGN This is the FIRST PHASE.

Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will meet with Owner to discuss Project Requirements. Surakshya's Design will Sign Agreement with the Owner for Design Services. Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will Analyze & Research project requirements to establish the framework of the project. Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will present a Detailed Program, Conceptual Notes and Schematic Design.

DESIGN This is the SECOND PHASE

Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will improve upon schematic design incorporating Owners Feedback Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will present Design Development (Illustrations, Videos, Photos, Notes). Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will sign agreement with Structural Consultants, MEP Consultants & HVAC Consultants Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will further improve on Design Development after input from Consultants. Surakshya's Design’s Architect Team will present A final Design Development after Reviewing to the Owner. Final Municpal Drawings, Working Drawings, Construction Documents, Material Specification, Cost Estimates is Presented.

CONSTRUCTThis is the THIRD (FINAL) PHASE.

After the DESIGN Phase is completed, Construction Process Begins. Surakshya's Design Can be involved in Total Construction Managment or Supervision Roles only as per owner’s needs.
